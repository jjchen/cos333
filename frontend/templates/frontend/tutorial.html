Hover over the tooltips to learn more about OC.

OC

  • OC's logo. Click here at any time to return to the splash page - i.e., this view.

Notifications

  • The arrow will glow red when you have been invited to an event, and clicking on it will show you what you have been invited to.

Personal Page

  • Click here to go to your personal page - add friends, accept invites, send invites, manage your events, and form and edit groups.

Settings

  • Modify your settings - change your name or default location.

Logout

  • Log out of OC. Default behavior is to stay logged in despite the page closing.

Make View

  • Make an event! Check Private to make the event visible to your selections in Groups. Add tags to make finding your event easier.

Calendar View

  • See what's happening today, this week, or this month in a calendar view. Filterable in the same way that the map is.

List View

  • See the events in list form - filterable in the way that the map and calendar are.

Collections

  • Three overarching collections of events. All shows all events, Recommended shows events your friends have created or are attending, and My Events shows any events you've created.

Tags

  • Shows all available tags and groups - click on one or any combination of them to show events corresponding your tag and group selection.

Search

  • Search events by title, location, or tags.

Single Event

  • There is only one event at this location. Click on it to show the event's information.

Multiple Events

  • There are two or more events at this location - click on the group to show all available events in spiral form, and click on a single person to show information regarding that event.
